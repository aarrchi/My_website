Positive Impact


  • Technology allows for working remotely, which has shown to increase employee productivity

  • Connecting with the best resources regardless of their location in the world

  • Helps save time, thanks to automation of redundant tasks

  • External and internal communication is quicker through emails, project management, co-working tools, and work place productivity apps

  • Technology has made medical commodities a hundred steps up to development.


Negative Impact


  • Technology and access to information in a central location has given rise to new kinds of crimes and terrorism

  • Reliance on technology has increased social anxiety, where people are more comfortable communicating with others through messages and chat apps, but not physically

  • Nuclear weapons and weapons of mass destruction

  • Technology is expenxive which cannot be afforded by everyone

  • It has made human dependent on it.
